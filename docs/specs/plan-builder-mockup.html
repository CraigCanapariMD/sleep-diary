Sleep Plan Builder

For clinicians and sleep coaches. Build a nightly plan, then hand the family a QR code or link — it loads straight into their Pediatric Sleep Diary. Nothing is sent to any server; the whole plan travels inside the link itself.

0 · Start from a protocol (optional)

Prefills everything below — then edit freely. The template is a starting point; the plan is yours. Pairs with a "what to expect" education pack shown night by night in the family's diary.

⚠ Chronotherapy is a supervised, ~1-week commitment: sleep moves around the clock, school/work need coordination, and relapse is likely without a strict maintenance plan (issue it as a follow-up plan at the review visit). A review date is required.
