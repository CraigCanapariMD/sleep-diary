FOR CLINICIANS & SLEEP PROFESSIONALS — families just scan the code you print

Sleep Plan Builder

Build a nightly sleep plan, then hand the family a QR code or link — it loads straight into their Pediatric Sleep Diary, shows tonight's targets while they log each morning, and reports adherence back on the diary's PDF. Nothing is sent to any server: the whole plan travels inside the link itself, and this page works offline once loaded.
